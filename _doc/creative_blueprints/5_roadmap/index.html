Post-MVP roadmap

Three high-impact capabilities shipping after the MVP — each one advancing the receive → store → ship loop with less manual effort and more operational clarity.

Outbound Order Management Dispatch

Supervisors create dispatch orders, pull pick lists tied to confirmed storage locations, and mark line items as shipped — closing the fulfillment loop from inbound receipt straight through to outbound dispatch without manual status chasing.

Live Inventory Dashboard Visibility

Real-time stock levels by zone, a location-utilization heatmap across aisles and bins, and proactive alerts for unassigned, overdue, or low-stock items — giving supervisors immediate situational awareness without querying individual records.

Barcode & QR Scan Support Automation

Mobile-friendly scan interface for receiving dock and dispatch. Inbound scans auto-log dock receipt and prompt storage assignment; dispatch scans confirm picks and update order status — eliminating manual data entry at both ends of the warehouse flow.
