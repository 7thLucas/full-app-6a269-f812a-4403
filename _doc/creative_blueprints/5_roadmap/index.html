What's Next

Post-MVP roadmap

Three high-impact capabilities shipping after the MVP — each one closing a remaining gap in the receive → store → ship loop.

Outbound Order Dispatch Board Dispatch

A dedicated supervisor queue for reviewing, prioritizing, and gate-confirming outbound orders before truck departure. Per-order status (pick pending → packed → ready), blocker flags, and explicit dispatch confirmation — closing the last mile of the fulfillment loop.

Inventory Alert System Proactive

Rule-based notifications surfaced in the dashboard — low-stock thresholds, unassigned storage locations, overdue inbound arrivals, and orders blocked by empty pick slots. Turns reactive radio calls into a structured, prioritized alert feed.

Shipment History & Audit Trail Traceability

Searchable, filterable log of every inbound and outbound status transition — timestamped, actor-attributed, and detail-tagged. Supports end-of-shift reviews, discrepancy investigations, and carrier performance tracking.
